GIS Dashboard

Your whole jurisdiction, on one live map.

The GIS Dashboard turns every record in the suite into a point on a live map. Complaints, assets, hoardings, works and NOCs appear as layers you can toggle, filter and analyse — so decisions are made by looking at the city, not at spreadsheets.
